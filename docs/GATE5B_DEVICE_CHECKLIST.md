# Gate 5B physical-device checklist

Install `WrathiOSGate5B-v6-unsigned.ipa` over the existing Gate 5A app. Do not
uninstall it: the unchanged bundle identifier must preserve the imported data.

1. Confirm the launcher detects the existing licensed import, then tap
   **Launch WRATH**.
2. Confirm the authentic animated WRATH menu appears and audio is still audible.
3. Touch down far from the current WRATH cursor. The cursor must not jump.
4. Drag slowly. The cursor must follow the finger delta predictably without
   continued motion after the finger stops.
5. Hold the finger still. Confirm the cursor stays still.
6. Lift, then touch down elsewhere. Confirm the cursor again does not jump.
7. Move the cursor over **Begin** and tap once. Confirm exactly one activation.
8. Return, move over **Options**, and tap once. Confirm exactly one activation.
9. Return or activate **Depart** once, as applicable, and confirm one action.
10. Drag across menu entries and lift. Confirm no entry activates.
11. On the main menu, background the app for three seconds and return once.
12. Confirm whether animation, input, GL output, and audio resume. Confirm the
    cursor is not moving, no button remains held, and the first new touch does
    not jump. If anything fails, record the exact visible frontier.
13. Capture one screenshot of the authentic menu after return and provide a
    short qualitative description of cursor movement, taps, and audio.

Do not begin gameplay testing. If possible after a failure, transcribe the
sanitized Gate 5 transcript stages for the background reset, foreground return,
and foreground first frame.
